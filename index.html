An Analysis of Premeir League Teams Over 4 Seasons

The Premeir League is an English Football League regarded as one of the best in the world. Every season 20 teams compete over the course of 9 months and 38 matches to be on top of the table and qualify for the UEFA Champions Leauge and the UEFA Europa League.

This project is an analysis of the 20 teams in the Permeir League over the course of 4 Seasons from the 2015/2016 season to 2018/2019. It will look at how team's total points over the 4 seasions, an analysis of the the top 4 and bottom 3 teams by points, and the correlation between different game statistics and total points.
